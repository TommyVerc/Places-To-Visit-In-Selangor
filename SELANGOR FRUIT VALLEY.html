If you love fruits, you should visit the Selangor Fruit Valley. Though there are other ‘fruit-related’ Selangor attractions, what makes this place stand out is its sheer size of 646 hectares.

Over here, you will get to indulge yourself with a variety of tropical fruits such as starfruit, guava, papaya, durians and many more; depending on what is in season.

You will also get to learn and distinguish the many different trees, herbs, and vegetables that are planted here.

    

LOCATION

    

Located: Pekan Sekinchan, 45400 Sekinchan, Selangor
District: Kuala Selangor
